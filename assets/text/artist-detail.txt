Malek — Biography:
Malek is a producer and performer whose work pulls from West African polyrhythms, electronic minimalism, and analogue synthesis. Trained in both traditional percussion and studio production, Malek's music balances visceral rhythm with lyrical introspection. His compositions have been featured in contemporary dance productions and independent film scores.
