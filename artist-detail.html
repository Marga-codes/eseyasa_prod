The Journey

Born into a lineage of percussionists in Lagos, Malek Azikiwe has redefined the boundaries of Afro-fusion. His work is characterized by "Asymmetrical Rhythms"—a technique that blends the heartbeat of the talking drum with modular synthesis.

Over the last decade, Malek has headlined festivals across three continents, earning him the title of 'The Modern Griot'. His performances are more than just concerts; they are immersive cultural rituals that bridge the gap between ancestral stories and digital futures.
